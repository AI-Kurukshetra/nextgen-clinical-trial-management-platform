What This Project Is About (ELI15)                                                                                                                                                          
                                                                                                                                                                                              
  ---                                                                                                                                                                                         
  The Problem
                                                                                                                                                                                              
  Imagine a pharmaceutical company like Pfizer wants to test a new medicine — say, a pill that might cure diabetes.
                                                                                                                                                                                              
  They can't just give it to people and hope for the best. The government (FDA) says: you must run a proper "clinical trial" — a carefully controlled experiment with real patients, doctors, 
  and hospitals.                                                                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                                                                                              
  Here's the thing: a single trial might involve...                                                                                                                                           
  - 50 hospitals across 20 countries
  - 2,000 patients                                                                                                                                                                            
  - 5 years of work                                         
  - Hundreds of doctors and nurses                                                                                                                                                            
  - Millions of documents                                   
                                                                                                                                                                                              
  Managing all of that with spreadsheets and emails? Total chaos.
                                                                                                                                                                                              
  ---                                                       
  What a CTMS Does                                                                                                                                                                            
                                                            
  A Clinical Trial Management System (CTMS) is basically the mission control software for running a drug trial.
                                                                                                                                                                                              
  Think of it like this:                                                                                                                                                                      
                                                                                                                                                                                              
  Clinical Trial  =  A massive rocket launch                                                                                                                                                  
  CTMS            =  NASA's mission control room                                                                                                                                              
   
  It tracks:                                                                                                                                                                                  
  - Which hospitals are participating and how they're doing 
  - How many patients have signed up vs. how many are needed                                                                                                                                  
  - Whether doctors are following the rules (the "protocol")
  - When someone breaks a rule — called a deviation — and what was done about it                                                                                                              
  - When inspectors visit each hospital to check everything is okay                                                                                                                           
  - Every single action anyone takes (for legal proof)                                                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                              
  ---                                                                                                                                                                                         
  What Veeva CTMS Is                                                                                                                                                                          
                                                                                                                                                                                              
  Veeva is a big company that sells this software to pharma companies. Their product costs hundreds of thousands of dollars per year. Think of it like Microsoft Office — but for running
  clinical trials, and way more expensive.                                                                                                                                                    
                                                            
  ---                                                                                                                                                                                         
  What We're Building                                       
                     
  We're building our own version of Veeva CTMS — a modern, open-source alternative — using:
                                                                                                                                                                                              
  - Next.js (the website/app framework — like the walls and windows of the building)                                                                                                          
  - Supabase (the database — like the filing cabinets that store all the data)                                                                                                                
  - React + TypeScript (the UI code — like the furniture and screens inside)                                                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                                                                              
  ---                                                                                                                                                                                         
  The 6 Core Things Our App Does                                                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                                                                                              
  1. Studies
  Create and manage each clinical trial. "We're running a Phase II trial for Drug X, targeting 500 patients across 20 sites."                                                                 
                                                                                                                                                                                              
  2. Sites                                                                                                                                                                                    
  Track each participating hospital. "Hospital A in New York has enrolled 45 out of their target 60 patients."                                                                                
                                                                                                                                                                                              
  3. Subjects (Patients)
  Log each patient anonymously (no real names — privacy law). "Patient 001-023 enrolled on March 5th, currently active."                                                                      
                                                                                                                                                                                              
  4. Monitoring Visits
  When an inspector (called a CRA) visits a hospital to check everything's being done correctly. Schedule it, track it, record what they found.                                               
                                                                                                                                                                                              
  5. Deviations
  When something goes wrong — a doctor gave the wrong dose, or missed a step. Log it, investigate why, fix it, close it.                                                                      
                                                                                                                                                                                              
  6. Dashboard                                                                                                                                                                                
  A big overview screen showing: how many patients are enrolled, which hospitals are behind, what problems are open, what visits are coming up.
